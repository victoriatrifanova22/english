The 6 tips — key ideas

Tip 1

Structure your working day

Tackle challenging tasks early so you don't end up working late or worrying overnight.

Tip 2

Schedule wind-down time

Make a to-do list for the next day before you log off — so it's off your mind in the evening.

Tip 3

Turn off notifications

Work emails and apps can wait. Android even lets you create a work profile to keep work apps separate.

Tip 4

Define home & work spaces

Especially for remote workers — put work stuff out of sight, out of mind when the day is done.

Tip 5

Create a mini routine

Change clothes, breathe, put on music — a small ritual helps you switch gears into personal time.

Tip 6

Pencil in some fun

Plan something to look forward to. It keeps you motivated and stops you from overthinking about work when you're off the clock.
