1. Presentation: Grammar & Vocabulary Refresh

Before we start playing, let's quickly review our main grammar rules.

Present Simple

We use it for habits and routines.

+I / You / We / They work.
He / She / It works.
-I don't work.
She doesn't work.
?Do you work?
Does he work?

Example: I wake up at 7 AM and go to the gym. She loves cats.

Verb "To Be"

We use it for names, age, feelings, and places.

+I am (I'm). He/She/It is.
You/We/They are.
-I am not. He isn't.
We aren't.
?Are you OK? Is she a teacher?

Example: I am 24 years old. My cat is very cute!

Have got

We use it to talk about possession and family.

+I have got ('ve got).
She has got ('s got).
-I haven't got.
He hasn't got.
?Have you got a pet?
Has she got a brother?

Example: I've got a lot of interesting science books.
