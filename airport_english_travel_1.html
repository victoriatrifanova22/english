Travel Quotes

Choose one quote that you like. Speak for 2–3 minutes using the plan below. Use words from the lesson to help you!

📋 Your Speaking Plan
1. Introduction — What does the quote mean? Say it in your own words. Do you agree with the idea at first glance?
2. Example from life — Tell a personal story, OR describe a character from a film or book who shows this idea. Use: This reminds me of… / For example… / I remember when… / In the film… the character…
3. Conclusion — Do you like this quote? Why? Would you share it with a friend? Use: To sum up… / Personally, I think… / Since then, I've realized… / This quote makes me want to…
