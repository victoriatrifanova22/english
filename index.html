Grammar

Must & Should

Review the rules, then practise below.

must

Obligation / necessity — it is required

  • You must show your passport at the border.
  • You must buy travel insurance before you go.
  • Passengers must fasten their seatbelts.
  • You must not take liquids over 100ml.

should

Advice / recommendation — it is a good idea

  • You should arrive two hours early.
  • You should try the local food — it's amazing!
  • You should take a bottle of water when hiking.
  • You shouldn't miss the sightseeing in the old town.

Speaking Tasks

Use these prompts to practise speaking. Aim for 4–6 sentences each. Try to use must, should, and the vocabulary from the lesson.

✈️ My Next Trip

Tell your teacher about a trip you are planning or dreaming about. Use the questions below as a guide.

Where do you want to go? Why this place? Have you been there before?
What must you do to prepare? (visa, travel insurance, packing…)
What should you see or do there? What is on your itinerary?
Where should you stay? What kind of accommodation are you thinking of?
What should you NOT miss — and what must you NOT forget to bring?
I'm planning to go to… I must book… / I should pack… My itinerary includes… I'd like to stay in… I shouldn't miss… I must remember to…
🎭 Role-play — missed flight

Student A (you): You missed your flight to Rome because your taxi was very late. You are now at the help desk. Explain what happened, ask about the next flight, and find out about accommodation for tonight.

Student B (teacher): You work at the airline help desk. Ask questions. Explain the rules using must.

Useful phrases for Student A

I'm afraid I missed my flight… There was a delay with my taxi… Could you tell me the next departure? Should I book new accommodation? What must I do now? Can I check my luggage in again? Do I need a new boarding pass? I have travel insurance — will that help?

Useful phrases for Student B

You must show your passport first. You must fill in this form. You should call your insurance company. The next departure is at… You must collect your luggage from… I'm afraid there's a delay on the next flight too.
