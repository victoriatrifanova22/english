🍽️ Food & Eating Out

Welcome to your interactive English lesson! Today we are learning food vocabulary, how to talk about quantities (some / any, much / many) and how to order food in a café.

1. Presentation: Useful Vocabulary & Phrases

Read the phrases below. Make sure you understand them before playing the games!

Food & drinks:
• fruit & vegetables 🍎🥕
• meat, fish & chicken 🍗
• rice, pasta & bread 🍞
• a cup of coffee / tea ☕
• a glass of water / juice 🧃
Talking about food you like:
• I love / like ...
• I don't really like ...
• I can't stand ...
• My favourite food is ...
• It's delicious / tasty / healthy.
Ordering in a café:
• Can I have a ..., please?
• I'd like ..., please.
• Anything to drink?
• How much is it?
• Here you are. / Enjoy your meal!
Grammar focus — quantity:
• some + ✅ (I'd like some bread.)
• any + ❓/❌ (Have you got any milk?)
• How much + uncountable (sugar)
• How many + countable (apples)

7. Production: Speaking Roleplay Cards

Hover over (or tap) the cards to reveal a situation. Imagine you are in this situation. What do you say? Practise out loud!

Situation 1
You are in a café. Order a sandwich and something to drink. Ask how much it is.
Situation 2
A friend asks: "What's your favourite food and why?" Answer with at least three sentences.
Situation 3
You want to make a pizza 🍕. Tell your partner what food you need. Use "some" and "any".
Situation 4
You are the waiter. Your partner is the customer. Take their order and recommend the soup of the day.
