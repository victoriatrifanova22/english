🩺 Feeling Under the Weather

Welcome to your interactive English lesson! Today we are learning how to talk about health problems and give advice.

1. Presentation: Useful Vocabulary & Phrases

Read the phrases below. Make sure you understand them before playing the games!

Asking about health:
• What's wrong?
• What's the problem?
• Are you OK?
• How are you today?
Saying you are not well:
• I don't feel well.
• I feel terrible.
• I've got a headache. (or a cold, a stomachache, a toothache, backache)
• My leg/arm hurts.
• My eyes hurt.
Answering & Giving Advice:
• Oh no! Poor you.
• I'm so sorry.
• Get well soon!
• It's a good idea to... (stay in bed / go to a doctor / take aspirin).

6. Production: Speaking Roleplay Cards

Hover over (or tap) the cards to reveal a situation. Imagine you are in this situation. What do you say? Practice out loud!

Situation 1
You are at work. Your back hurts a lot. Tell your boss you need to go home.
Situation 2
Your friend calls you to go to the cinema. You have a terrible toothache. What do you say?
Situation 3
Your cat 🐱 looks sick and you think it has a stomachache. Call the vet (animal doctor) and explain the problem.
Situation 4
