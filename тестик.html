✈️ Travel & Troubles (Roleplays)

Read the situation and explain what you would do. Use Must or Should and the required vocabulary.

Situation 1: The Airport

You are at the check-in desk. The staff tells you your luggage is 5kg overweight. You don't want to pay extra.

Required Words: realize change my mind should

"When I realized my bag was too heavy, I..."
"I should probably..."

Situation 2: The Disaster

You are travelling to a very well-known destination. Suddenly, there is a 6-hour delay. You have no accommodation booked yet.

Required Words: anxious must rely on

"I feel very anxious because..."
"I must rely on..."

Situation 3: The Advice

Your friend is going abroad for the first time. They want a perfect itinerary but they get tired easily.

Required Words: sightseeing take a lot of time should not

"You shouldn't plan too much sightseeing because..."

🤔 Would You Rather...?

Explain your choice in detail. Give at least two reasons.

Choice A

Would you rather have a fully planned itinerary but no free time, OR just wander around sightseeing with no plan at all?

Choice B

Would you rather take care of a friend's pet for a month, OR deal with a 12-hour flight delay?

Choice C

Would you rather visit a well-known crowded tourist spot, OR an unknown place where you can't keep in touch with anyone (no internet)?

🗣️ Useful Phrases to start your answer:

  • "I'd rather choose the first option because..."
  • "For me, the second option is more interesting because..."
  • "I've always dreamed of..."
  • "The other option sounds nice, but..."
