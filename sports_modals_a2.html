📖 Presentation

Grammar Reference Card

MUST

Rules, obligations, strong advice

  • You must wear a helmet.
  • Swimmers must follow the rules.
Subject + must + verb

CAN / CAN'T

Ability, possibility, permission

  • You can swim here.
  • I can't climb those rocks.
Subject + can/can't + verb

MUSTN'T

Prohibition – do NOT do this!

  • You mustn't litter in the forest.
  • Hikers mustn't leave without water.
Subject + mustn't + verb
