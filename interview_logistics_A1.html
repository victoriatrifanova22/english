6. Roleplay: HR Interview Simulation

Hover your mouse over the cards (or tap them). Read the task and speak out loud. Try to speak for 30-60 seconds.

👋Card 1:
Introduction
"Tell me about yourself."

Say your name, where you are from, and your current job.
📋Card 2:
Past Experience
"What were your main responsibilities?"

Name 3 things you did every day in your last job (use Past Simple!).
🚀Card 3:
Motivation
"Why did you leave your last job?"

Give 1 or 2 good reasons. Tell them what you want now.
💪Card 4:
Strengths
"What are your strengths?"

Tell them what you are good at (e.g. communication, time management, working with people).
