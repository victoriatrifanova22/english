My school & university life
Use the blocks below to build your story. Speak for 1–2 minutes!
🏫 Where?
My school was in your city.
It was a big / small school.
There were about ___ students.
📅 When?
I was at school from ___ to ___.
I was ___ years old.
It wasn't very long ago!
😊 How was it?
School was
amazingfunboring difficultinterestinghard
My favourite subject was ___.
Maths / English / PE wasn't my favourite.
👩‍🎓 You as a student
I was a good / quiet / funny student.
I wasn't very fast at reading.
My grades were good / ok / not great.
💬 Sample monologue — read & adapt

My school was in Incheon. It was a big school — there were about 800 students. I was at school from 2005 to 2018. My favourite subject was art. I was quite a good student, but maths wasn't easy for me!

👩‍🏫 Teachers
My favourite teacher was ___.
He / She was kind / strict / funny / young.
The lessons were always interesting.
He / She wasn't boring at all!
👫 Friends
My best friend was ___.
We were in the same class.
He / She was very funny / smart / positive.
There were 5 of us in our group.
🗣️ Useful phrases
Also, my teacher was very patient.
But sometimes the homework was too hard.
I think school was a great time.
🎨 Adjectives to use
kindstrictfunny smartstrongamazing famouspositiverichpoor
💬 Sample

My favourite teacher was Mrs Kim. She was very kind and funny. Her lessons were never boring! My best friend was Jisoo. We were in the same class for three years. She was amazing at maths but I wasn't!

🎓 Where & what?
I was a student at ___ University.
My subject was ___.
The university was in ___.
There were many international students.
📚 Student life
The lectures were long but interesting.
My dormitory was small but cosy.
We weren't rich — we were students!
The canteen food was ok / amazing / terrible.
🌟 Best & worst
The best part was ___.
The worst part was the exams!
My group was very friendly.
The professors were strict but fair.
💭 Your opinion
I think university was the best time.
It was hard but very interesting.
I wasn't ready for the exams!
But I was happy there.
💬 Sample

I was a student at Seoul University. My subject was business. The lectures were long but I was very interested. My group was great — we were all very different people. The best part was our trips together. The worst part was the exams — I wasn't ready at all!

⭐ Who?
My hero was ___.
He / She was a famous ___.
singerathleteactor teacherscientistfootballer
🌟 Why a hero?
He / She was very strong / smart / kind.
He / She was the best in the world.
He / She wasn't rich at first, but…
His / Her story was amazing.
💚 Why important to you?
I was a big fan when I was young.
He / She was my inspiration.
I wasn't the same person after…
For me, he / she was the best person.
🔗 Connectors
Also, he / she was very humble.
But life wasn't easy for them.
And they were always positive.
So for me they were amazing.
💬 Sample

My hero was Roger Federer. He was the best tennis player in the world. His story was amazing — he wasn't rich at the beginning but he was always positive and strong. I was a big fan when I was at school. For me, he was the best person in sport.

🎤 Now it's YOUR turn!

Use all 4 blocks (school → teachers & friends → university → hero) to make your own monologue. Try to speak for 1–2 minutes. Use was / were / wasn't / weren't in every part!
